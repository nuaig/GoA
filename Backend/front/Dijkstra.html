Algorithm

        DIJKSTRA(G, w, s):
        1.  INITIALIZE-SINGLE-SOURCE(G, s)
        2.  S = ∅
        3.  Q = G.V
        4.  while Q ≠ ∅:
        5.      u = EXTRACT-MIN(Q)
        6.      S = S ∪ {u}
        7.      for each vertex v ∈ Adj[u]:
        8.          RELAX(u, v, w)
